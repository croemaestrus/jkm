About the app

With BEEPER, you will experience a new way to chat, share files and enjoy your work + social life...all this while maintaining the online privacy that you very much value!

Who

Three males of African descent came together and instead of hunting they decided to easen how we communicate. They used multiple creative functionalities, unique features, easy to use designs and decided to give the world this amazing creation.

Why

Relishing the experience every time you use this app you'd probably answer this question as WHY NOT!!! Beeper is the best fit for anyone who considers the need to maintain an interactive, secure and absolutely easy to use app. This is it. This is the path.

How

Beeper is designed to actively and spontaneously synchronize your chats whether you are using your phone or your computer with added features that enhance security, connectivity and ease. Be assured that the heartbeat of the conversations never stops

Key Features

Beeper is here to change how you chat, change how you send files, change your work and social life..all this while maintaining the privacy that you very much value. With our collection of features that let you engage in conversation from your phone or desktop, you will be able to send and receive files of up to 2GB - whether it's a bunch of documents or photos, all are included.

Sync. your chats

Ability to concurrently chat from any device means your Beeper will automatically update its conversations on every device where you have an account. Whether it is your computer, tablet, phone... The conversation never stops!

Secure. Communication

Security doesn't come in any better way of not through Beeper. You can secure a particular conversation with different security options, from a passcode, to hiding it from view of anyone who gains access to your account.

Socialize. with Freedom

Beeper makes it easy for you to manage your social life. With functionalities that allow you to pre-record and send chats or files at a specific time in the future, allows you to create your account either using your phone number or email address, plus much more. Get your Beeper now to discover.
